[[https://www.kaggle.com/c/tpu-getting-started/overview/faq][Petals to the Metal]] is Kaggle's newest [[https://www.kaggle.com/c/tpu-getting-started/overview/faq][Getting Started]] competition, highlighting *Tensor Processing Units*. A TPU is an accelerator developed by Google especially for machine learning. They can be used in TensorFlow much like GPUs, and are quite powerful. I had the pleasure of writing a tutorial to go along with the competition. Check it out!
